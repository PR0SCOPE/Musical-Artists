This is a Famous Song by Ariana Grande Called: 7 Rings





Ariana Grande was born June 26, 1999. She is an American singer, songwriter, and actress. From 2010 to 2013, she played Cat Valentine on the Nickelodeon sitcom Victorious. She played that role again on the spin-off Sam & Cat. In 2013, she released her first studio album, Yours Truly. It became her first number one album in the United States. Her second studio album, My Everything, was released on August 25, 2014. Her third studio album, Dangerous Woman, was released May 20, 2016. In 2019, Grande released her fifth studio album Thank U, Next. In 2020, she released her sixth studio album Positions. In 2021, Grande was the new coach in the first season of The Voice.
